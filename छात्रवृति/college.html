Mukhyamantri Yuva Swavalamban Yojana

Mukhya Mantri Yuva Swavalamban Yojana (MYSY) scholarship is a scholarship program offered by the government of Gujarat, India for students pursuing higher education. The scholarship is aimed at providing financial assistance to students from economically and socially disadvantaged backgrounds to help them pursue higher education. The scholarship is open to students who are enrolled in undergraduate and postgraduate courses in recognized universities and colleges in Gujarat.

Eligibility requirements:

  • Students must have secured 80 or more Percentile in 12th Science (PCB).
  • Family income must be less than Rs. 6 lakh/annum.
  • Have taken valid addmition.

Benefits

  • Students can apply for buying books and educational support
  • Students can get INR 10 lakh who are in Medical Education.
  • Students of all categories will get an age relaxation of 5 years.
  • The government will also provide financial assistance of INR 1,200 per month for 10 months to students who are in a region.
  • All the students who have passed Class 10 and 12 with 80% marks and have opted for diploma courses will receive financial aid of INR 25,000 per year or 50% of the course fees.
  • The government will also provide free-of-cost outfits, reading material, etc. to the students under the MYSY scholarship scheme.

Application deadline: 30-July-2023

Documents

  • Income certificate
  • HSc/ SSC marks card from the Gujarat Board
  • Address Proof such as Aadhar card/ Ration card
  • Passport size photograph
  • Resident certificate
  • Bank account details
  • Copy of Admission Receipt

Food Bill Assistance For Medical/Engineering Students (SEBC), Gujarat 2022-23

Food Bill Assistance for Medical/Engineering Students (SEBC), Gujarat 2022-23 invites applications from medical and engineering students. The scheme aims to provide monthly stipend against hostel food bills for students belonging to socially and economically backward classes.

Eligibility requirements:

  • This scheme is applicable for the students studying in the Medical and Engineering colleges and in college affiliate hostels.
  • The annual family income of the applicant must be below INR 2.50 Lac from all sources.

Benefits

  • Deserving students will get INR 1,250 per month as meal bill relief.

Application deadline: 30-July-2023

Documents

  • Self-attested caste certificate issued by the competent authorities
  • Self-attested copy of the Aadhaar Card
  • The first page of bank passbook/cancelled cheque
  • Fee receipt of current academic course
  • Self-attested certificate of previous academic mark sheet
  • Break affidavit (If break gap is more than one year)
  • Hostel certificate (only for hosteller student)
  • Income certificate issued by a competent authority (Form no.16 required for Govt. Employee)
  • School leaving certificate
  • School/College current year bonafide certificate

Post-Matric Scholarships Gujarat For Minorities 2023

Gujarat CM Post Matric Scholarship 2023 is funded by government of Gujarat which is offering different schemes for the students under minority, considering their excellence in the studies, these candidates who has good academics can apply for the present scholarships, these scholarships are provided by the Higher Education Department of Gujarat, to be eligible for the scheme the students were to been studying in any degree course, by giving scholarships to the students of OBC, SC, ST and minorities government want to develop the status of the minorities and to give encouragement to the students with good academic excellence.

Eligibility requirements:

  • The scholarship will be given from class 11th to Ph.D. level.
  • The 30 % scholarship is reserved for the girl candidates of a minority community.
  • Only the students of the minority community can apply for this scholarship.
  • Annual Income must not exceed more than 2 Lac.

Benefits

  • The selected candidate will get an Admission fee of INR 10,000 PA to INR 7000 PA (depending on course) and a maintenance allowance up to INR 1200 PM for 10 continuous months (depending on course).

Application deadline: 31-Aug-2023

Documents

  • Domicile certificate for the respective state
  • Self-declaration of minority community certificate by the student
  • Self-attested copy of previous academic mark sheet
  • Copy of Aadhar Enrollment/Aadhar Card
  • Income certificate issued by designated state/UT authority
  • Proof of bank account in the name of the student or joint account with mother/father
