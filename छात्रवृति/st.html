The Cadence Scholarship Program 2023-24

Eligibility requirements:

  • Open for candidates residing and studying in Delhi NCR, Bangalore, Pune & Ahmedabad only (Indian nationals only).
  • For Undergraduate course: Have successfully passed class 12 with a minimum overall score of 60%. Candidates who are currently studying in class 12 can also apply on the basis of their previous records.
  • For Postgraduate course: Have successfully completed graduation or are in the final year of their graduation program.
  • Candidates must come from a lower-income group.

NOTE:

  • Students who are already pursuing graduation or postgraduation courses are not eligible
  • Students pursuing STEM courses, girls, transgenders, and persons with disabilities are encouraged to apply.

Benefits

Financial support for key educational expenses like course fees, hostel charges, books, and conveyance (in exceptional cases).

Application deadline:31-Mar-2023

Documents

  • Details of two references who have agreed to give a recommendation (They must not be related to the applicants and should have direct knowledge of their character)
  • Recent passport-size photograph of the applicant

How can you apply?

Step 1: Click on the 'Apply Now' below.

Step 2: Fill in the details and submit the application form.


Pre Matric Scholarship for ST Students 2023

Eligibility requirements:

  • Provider details: Ministry of Tribal Affairs, Government of India
  • Eligibility: ST students of Class 9 and 10
  • Award details: Up to Rs.350 per month and books/ ad-hoc grant of up to Rs.1,000 per annum
  • .Application mode: Online through the National Scholarship Portal
  • Application timeline: Between January and March (tentative)

NOTE:

  • The ST category students who are domiciles of Tripura state can avail benefits under this scholarship scheme for their studies in class 9 and 10.
  • The annual family income should not be more than Rs 2 lakh from all sources.

Benefits

The key objective of this scholarship is to support the studies of needy students belonging to ST category.

Application deadline:31-Mar-2023
