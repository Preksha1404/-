Vidyadhan Scholarship

Sarojini Damodaran Foundation has created the Vidyadhan Scholarship to help the students who are currently studying in Classes 11th and 12th. 5000 rupees per annum will be provided to the students belonging to the prescribed classes.

Eligibility requirements:

  • The applicant should have scored at least 90% marks or equivalent in Class 10/ SSc examination.
  • Disabled candidates should have scored at least 75% marks in Class 10 qualifying examination.
  • The students should have completed their Class 10 education from Kerala, Karnataka, Pondicherry, Tamil Nadu, Telangana, Andhra Pradesh, Gujarat, and Maharashtra.
  • The annual family income of the applicant should not exceed INR 2,00,000 from all sources.

Benefits

  • The selected students belonging to Gujarat will receive a scholarship amount of INR 5000 per annum to pursue Class 11 and Class 12.
  • Students pursuing graduate-level programs will receive a scholarship amount of INR 10,000 - INR 60,000 per annum based on the course, duration of the course, state, etc.

Application deadline: 31-July-2023

Documents

  • SSLC/ SSC/ Class 10 or equivalent marks card (If the original marks card is not available, the students can upload the provisional marks card from the respective State/ CBSE/ ICSE board website)
  • Passport size photograph
  • Income certificate
  • Disability certificate (if applicable)

CBSE Single Girl Child Scholarship 2023

The only objective of the government behind starting this scholarship is to promote girl child education in the country, so that every capable student can get the benefit of education. On the path of fulfilling this objective, CBSE has released CBSE Single Girl Child Scholarship. There are many such girl students across the country, who despite being promising are not able to complete their education up to class X due to financial constraints.

Eligibility requirements:

  • Students applying under the scholarship must be a woman, the only child of their parents & permanent residents of India.
  • To take advantage of this scholarship, such children will also be eligible, who are twins together and more than this. Apart from these, any other child in the family will not be eligible.
  • All students must be in class XI or XII in a school affiliated under the Central Board of Secondary Education.
  • Tuition for the students studying in the present school should not exceed Rs. 1500 per month.
  • To take advantage of CBSE Single Girl Child Scholarship, the applicant must have passed class X with at least 60% marks.
  • The only difference for these students is that the school tuition fee for them should not exceed Rs 6,000 per month.

Benefits

  • Eligible students studying in class 11th will be provided Rs 500 per month under this facility, which will be Rs 6000 per year.
  • Along with this, students who are pursuing their education in class 12th will also be provided Rs 500 per month under this scholarship. This amount will also be Rs 6000 for the year.
  • Students applying through CBSE Single Girl Child Scholarship will get a total scholarship of Rs 12000 for 2 years.
  • This scholarship benefit amount will prove to be very beneficial for the student who is financially weak and unable to pursue his education.

Application deadline: 30-Nov-2022

Documents

  • Family income certificate
  • Caste certificate
  • Proof of admission
  • Bank account details
  • Fee Structure Details
  • Passport size photo,Scanned signature
  • Link aadhar card with bank account
  • An affidavit duly assisted by the SDM or First Class Magistrate or Gazette Officer, not below the rank of Tehsildar, on a stamp paper of Rs 50 from the parents or girls, mentioning that she is the only child in the family Is
