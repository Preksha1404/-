Tuition Fees and Examination Fees to OBC Students, Maharashtra 2022-23

Eligibility requirements:

  • be a resident of Maharashtra
  • belong to OBC category
  • be pursuing studies at post-matric level (Class 11 to PhD) in a government/government-aided/private institution
  • have an annual family income of less than or equal to INR 8 lakhs

Benefits

The selected scholars will reimbursement for compulsory fees like tuition fees, exam fees, and other admissible fees.

Application deadline:31-Mar-2023

Documents

  • Caste certificate issued by a competent authority
  • Income certificate/Income declaration issued by a competent authority
  • Caste validity certificate (for professional courses)
  • Mark sheet of HSC or SSC or last examination
  • Gap certificate
  • Leaving certificate
