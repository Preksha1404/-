SC Post Matric Scholarship 2023

Full tuition fee (including non-refundable fee): Rs 2 lakh per annum for students studying in private sector institutions and Rs 3.72 lakh per annum for private sector flying club

Eligibility requirements:Applicant must belong to the SC category

  • Must be a member of the SC caste.
  • Must have a minimum GPA of 3.0.
  • Must be enrolled in a full-time undergraduate or graduate program.

Application deadline: May 1, 2023

Top Class Education Scheme for SC Students

The Scheme will operate in all the institutions notified by the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment for FY 2020-21 only, subject to extension of the Scheme beyond the indicated period

Eligibility requirements:Those SC students having total annual family income from all sources up to Rs. 8.00 lakh and having secured admission in a full-time prescribed course in any of the notified institutions

  • Must be a member of the SC caste.
  • Must be enrolled in a full-time undergraduate or graduate program.
  • Must demonstrate financial need.

Application deadline: June 1, 2023
